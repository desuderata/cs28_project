Help Page

Introduction
Not that it matters. You won't read it anyways.

Just a year ago, you were toiling in front of your screen, jabbing away at keyboard deciding which student to fail and on what account; all these while paying Microsoft their costly annual subscription for a few weeks worth of editing an excel spreadsheet. That's why you came up to us, to streamline the failings (touch wood) without knocking a chunk out of your department budget.

View Course Grades

Course grades — responsible for more sleepless nights than insomnia. Here's where you view your worst nightmares. You could even sort them in increasing or decreasing order.

Manage

Missing grades? Discretionary zones? Passing (hopefully) and failing is just a few clicks away. You could edit and export individual user grades and Degree Classification. Plus, the rows are highlighted to help you identify issues so even the luckiest of your students wouldn't slip by to get an undeserving pass.

Upload

Yes, that's great and all but how could you get those pesky data into the database? That's what the upload page is for. The name is self explanatory.

This page is for looking up different courses and the grades of the students taking those courses.
The user is able to filter what year is shown from the drop down menu 'Year' above the table and you can sort the tables in asending or desending order from the small arrows next to the column title.
Users also have the option to search for any specific information (i.e. student number, course code) they want from the table using the search box on the right side above the table. Next to the search box there is the option to change the view of the data, go into fullscreen or toggle what data is shown in the table.
